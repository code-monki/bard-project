Princess Allianah Far Trader

design by Lewis Roberts

From the outside the Princess Allianah class looks exactly like an Empress Marva class far trader. It is only when you get inside the engine room and see the size of the jump engines that you realize that this isn't your average tramp freighter. The class was designed by Solomani Security (SolSec) for use as a secret courier in Imperial Space. Several dozen of these ships were active in the rimward sectors of the Imperium.

The ship was designed with SSDS as presented in Starships.
