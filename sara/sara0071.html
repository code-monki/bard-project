Beaufort Fighter

design by Drifter Poolside

Ok, heres a quick and dirty 1st attempt at a fighter. Kept it small and tech 14, because itll be easier to go bigger and higher tech. Basic idea was for a long range skirmisher, keeping in the 10-20 hex range from targets. This was designed with Antti Lahtinens spreadsheets, but its "basic" FF&S (so no power plant scaling etc). The things damn expensive, but its hard to lock, quick, and has a few options to allow for a range of offensive defensive stances. The lasers are optimised for a 20 hex range, and "feed" from a central HPG. Only one beam pointer used, as i assumed all were slaved to the same target. Feel free to flame away, im off to build a mini turreted version, and play with Peter Gray's ideas on gunboats.
