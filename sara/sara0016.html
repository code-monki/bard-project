Tacitus Class System Defense Boat

design by Unknown

The Tacitus is an Imperial design widely produced during the 5th Frontier War. It was designed primarily as an export comodity to allow lower tech level worlds to supplement their Shukugan Squadrons. As the Tacitus is designed at TL-14, extensive training and support programs were offered as part of the Tacitus sales package.
