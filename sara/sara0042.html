Manoah Fuel Pack

design by Nick Bradbeer

Here's the Samson's dedicated fuel skimmer/power pack. When attached, the Manoah provides jump and manoevre fuel, as well as a considerable whack of power for module systems. The craft can also run its drives while attached to the clipper, should extra power be required due to heavy loading or an emergency situation.
