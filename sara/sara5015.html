Starburst for Extreme Heroism Class Light Cruiser

design by John Macpherson

The Starburst for Extreme Heroism Cruiser is the standard Imperial light cruiser. It is used for a variety of tasks, from escort duty, piracy supression, to strikes against lightly defended planetary systems. The ship is named for recipients of the Starburst for Extreme Heroism, the highest military honor the Imperium grants. The ship is also known as the SEH or Starburst.
