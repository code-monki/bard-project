Dugan-Class Shuttle

design by Lewis Roberts

The Dugan-Class Shuttle is built at Dushgin & Delgado's lunar shipyard. It is designed to ferry around the various modules that the Aurora family of ships uses. If it does not mount a module, it easily has enough thrust to rip itself to shreds. Saftey overides are installed, but Dushgin & Delgado's takes no responsiblity for anyone overiding the saftey overides. It looks like a miniature version of an Aurora. A small compartment up front, a long spine, and a rear compartment holding engineering and the engines.
