Bark*ktonye-Class Light Fighter

design by TravlerTNE

The Bark*ktonye Class Light Fighter (Hiver Designation: FN-ILF-B2 Mk I) is the 11 ton skirmishing anti-fighter fighter used by the Hive Federation Navy. Being very light, it doesn't possess any missles or MFD. Its weaponry consists solely of a 350Mj Non-Grav-focused Xray Laser Lance and is powered to fire at over 3 shots per minute. One of the main uses of the Bark*ktonye is point-defense fighter screen for capital ships and light recon missions where heavy opposition isn't expected. They're are not as common as the more useful S*kfani and F'naah class Medium and Heavy Fighters but are more common in the interior of the Hive Federation, on routine patrol of Federation Navy bases and secure areas, areas that rate a patrol but don't expect heavy opposition. There is only a crew of one Ithklur for the Bark*ktonye.
