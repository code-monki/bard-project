Tri ceratops Heavy Fighter

design by Robert Boleyn

Here is my attempt at a 50-ton non-jump capable fighter, for your enjoyment/amusement. Comments are welcome, as this is my first attempt at a small craft design (for some reason ships with 40GW+ PAWS just seem more exciting :)

I designed the Triceratops using acceleration based on mass, and using Antti's newest spreadsheet, so some details probably don't quite match FF&S e.g. the hull uses power.
