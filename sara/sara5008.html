Patton Class Troop Transport

design by Unknown

The Patton class transport was original designed by Jillen ship yard in 1037 as a transport for a reinforced battalion. The Patton was given enough speed and armament for operate simi-independently in low to middle level theat areas. Never popular with the Imperial Navy, the Patton class was produced on and off, in small numbers, for the next 125 years by various companies th ought the imperium. The majority of the Pattons produced served with local system navies.
