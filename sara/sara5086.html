Ngihrurek Light Meson Cruiser

design by Yves Simon

The Ngihrurek is a relatively new design. In 1182 the Confederation navy needed a small and versatile ship that could be used to fill several different rols, from planetary bombardement to fleet escort (protecting battleships against small threats so it can concentrate on the big boys) and even long range patrol or missile barrage. It is a ship of average speed (only 4G of accelleration) but sporting a good protection (25 cm of BSD, 20 sandcasters, 20 Nuclear Dampers, 20 PDS Laser arrays and a 1000AV Meson screen). The main characteristic of this ship is the use of modulable bays (2 of 50dTons & 2 of 100dTons of size). This modularity allows the ship to easily shift from one role to another and to replace the secondary weapons in case of battle damage. Fixed weaponery is also quite impressive (a 25Gj Spinal PAW and 50 laser turrets). The powerplant can provide enough power to use the most energy hungry 'battle pods' with shortage (but Contra-Grav should not be used) and with non-energy based Battle Pods, the ship can overpower it's laser turrets to achieve a higher ROF

The available Battle Pods are:

    50dTons 5x720 Mj Laser
    50dTons Missile pod (containing 48 missiles)
    50dTons Hangar (with a single laser turret and a 20dTons light fighter)
    100dTons 5.6Gj PAW
    100dTons 4 Gj Meson gun (range is quite short so the main use is for planetary bombardement)
    100dTons Missile pod (containing up to 98 standard missiles)
    100dTons Hangar (with a single 380 Mj laser barbette, two 20dTons light fighters and a small 3 man flight deck)

A few other pods are being developped:
    50dTons PAW
    50dTons EW pod
    50dTons Marines assault Pod (containing equipment to service and launch drop capsules)
    100dTons EW pod
    100dTons Marines assault Pod (containing equipment to service and launch drop capsules)

Cargo pods of both size are also available along with pods designed to transport troops or passengers.

This is a variant of the CL-15 Ngihrurek-Class Light Cruiser wich replace the two 100 dTons PAW Bays by Meson Bays. The meson is quite short ranged and is better used agains light units of for ground bombardments. Except for those modifications, there is only few changes
