Okhtyoui Assualt Lander

design by Yves Simon

This is the daughter craft of the Waorlasa'Eakhko Troop Transport. This ship is not jump capable, but can manage up to 5G. All the craft is built around one major goal: Landing on a given planet and unload up to 300 troops and 30 Grav APCs. Armor is good for a ship of this size (15 cm of BSD), All system are doubled for redundancy, ECM and EMM are fitted.

Weaponry is composed of four 750Mj Laser Lances, ten 225Mj Laser Barbettes and 4 Missiles Barbettes. Defensive capabilities are provided by 8 Sancaster Turrets, a couple of Nuclear Damper Turrets and 6 PDS laser Turrets.

Accommodation are only provided for Crew, troops are only allocated seats. Due to low available Hangar space on the mother ship, Part of the MarCav vehicles are stored in the Okhtyoui Lander, although all the troops are housed in the mothership. Also recreationnal areas are low (only 50 LSR worth), the crew often share those of the mothership.
