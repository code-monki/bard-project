Zozgimayrehrih Battleship

design by Yves Simon

The Zozgimayrehrih-Class Battleship is an heavily armored Battleship capable of J-4 and 3G of acceleration. Its offensive armement is composed of a large 720 Gj PAW, 150 180-Mj Laser Turrets and 300 240-Mj Laser Barbettes. Along with its two 100dT Missiles bays, this allows the BH-16 to deliver a deadly barrage capable of damaging even the largest existing Dreadnough (although dreadnough larger than 700 000 dT would suffer only limited damage). On smaller (Cruiser) ship, this would allow to cripple such a ship in one volley. The defensive armement is quite impressive also: 100 Sandcatser turrets and 100 small Point Defense laser arrays (5 lasers per array), capable of dealing with missiles and small crafts. Passive defense is composed of a Meson shield of almost 4000 MW (2000 AV) and 90 centimeters of Bonded Superdense armor plating. Several Jammers & Decoys are installed.

The battleship also transport 12 assault shuttles for boarding actions, 8 space shuttles and 2 squadrons of heavy fighters (40 total).

Crew accomodations are good for such a ship, all crew members are accomodated 2 per small state rooms & officers 1 per SSR. There is also space for up to 1000 more marines troops if necessary (500 SSR) and recreationnal areas are quite large and well furnished (There is even a sauna and a swimming pool). A large complement of frozen watch allows the ship to cope with abttle damage (loss of crewmembers) without any decreases in efficiency, the 5 000 low berths and the crew are attended by a large infirmary.

All communication systems are duplicated for back-up purpose, the ship having 5 Fiber-optic computers. A large lab depatment is present to help study in Virus encuntered.

The ship is capable of Jump 4, as more than 9 hours of maneuvers at full power (add 1.5 hour per parsec worth of jump fuel), and has contra-gav lifters. It is capable of atmospheric reentry (although this put great stress on the structure) and can refuel on a gas giant withou difficulties. it takes a little more than 3 hours to refuel and almost 40 to process the fuel.

The ship is capable of leading a large fleet as it holds a 70 places Flag Bridge. All of come with a price, the ship is quite heavy and can only manage 3G of accelleration.

The first ship of this class was commisionned some time before the Maghiz, based on Solomani designs, these ships looks quite aggressive. At the time of the Maghiz, only a few of them did survive the setback (they were mothballed in the Oort Cloud at that time). Three of them where reused after the Set-Back, all the other serving as spare parts for those 3 lucky. In 1216, the Darrian Confederation sent a small fleet in the Wild to try to find and recontact any pockets of civilization. They did found more than they expected: a whole civilization of Virus-Infected ships. Running back to warn the Rengency, only one of them did managed to come back: the Notan Taledh (named after one of the most famous and almost mythical phiosopher around -22 000).

Badly damaged in the wilds, the Notan Taledh was not repairable (too much structural damage). It was then decided to rebuild it using the hull of one of it sister ship still in the Oort Cloud (the Yikan Yikan). Using the hull of the second and most intact parts from the first, after ordering some missing parts to other shipyards, the Notan Taledh was resurrected and assigned to the 1st Assault Squadron as its new flagship.
