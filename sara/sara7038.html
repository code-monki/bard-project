Naasirka Darusha Liner

design by Peter Gray

The former Vilani Megacorp Naasirka specialised primarily in the manufacturing of electronics and heavy manufactured goods like vehicles, but the company also possessed a sizeable shipping line that ran between Rhylanor/Spinward Marches and Vland. This line ran a mixture of both cargo and passengers Behind the Claw, its ships usually following the main X-Boat routes. However several contractor firms ran feeders lines from off of these routes in Corridor and Trailing Deneb. The workhorse of this trade was the the 800 ton Darusha Liner, an otherwise basic J-4 and 1G vessel that carried mostly passengers with a little bit of cargo.

While now a very rare vessel in Regency service, it is quite common in many of the coreward sectors that Naasirka served. A couple of examples serve with the Souk free trader collective, though many more are found in independent service with lucky free traders elsewhere, or derelict throughout Corridor and Provence.
