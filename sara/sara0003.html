Cutlass-Class System Patrol Boat

design by Lewis Roberts

The Cutlass-Class was built using lessons learned in the designing the Hobnail-class SDB. Its designers thought it was a bit to small to be called a System Defense Boat, so the type designation System Patrol Boat was created. The Cutlass-Class has the same performance as the Hobnail-Class, but without the planetary bombardment facilities, the Configuarable Pod, and a smaller cargo bay. This allows the ship to be built on a smaller hull and for considerably less money.

The Cutlass-Class is designed purely for the export market. The salespeople pitch the ship as a small highly armed ship capable of patrolling a system. It won't take out a Vampire Capital ship, but is more than capable of fighting off a pirate, smuggler or rebel ship.

While the ship is streamlined, it doesn't not normally enter planetary atmospheres. Its fusion engines would contaminate the biosphere of any planet. It normally only enters the atmosphere of gas giants. Since the ship can not land on the planet's surface to pick up crew and supplies, these are normally supplied by surface-to-orbit shuttles. If the planet does not have the capacity to build such vehicles, the Oriflamme have several models that clients can purchase.
