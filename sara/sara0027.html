Spacehawk-Class Heavy Fighter

design by TravelrTNE

The pilot manuevers the Spacehawk and controls the laser lances. The Sensors Intercept Officer, or SIO, controls the Sensors, MFD, missle fire and electronics.

The Spacehawk 50 ton heavy fighter is designed to give the Regency Navy Space Superiority and keep Virus out of civilization. Capable of an astounding 7Gs and carrying 10 standard space missles and 2, 750Mj Xray laser lances, the Spacehawk is fast and deadly. The mandate of the Regency was a craft of 50 tons displacement that could both adequetly perform screening for fleet elements as well as being able to perform long duration patrols.

The Spacehawk is cloaked from enemy observation by state of the art EM Masking and possesses an 300,000km EMS Jammer. Possession of an integral Radio Reciever along w/ 2, 1000AU Maser Comms and a backup 1000AU laser comm ensure communications capability. Two TL-15 Model St Computers control the craft . Detection and Fire Control Locks being vital to its mission, the Spacehawk has a powerful 480,000km AEMS suite and a 5m fixed 90,000km PEMS as well as a 300,000km LADAR.

Defense is not slighted either. With a hull of Bonded Superdense 3.214 cm thick, the Spacehawk can shrug off enemy fire and close for the kill. Crew comfort is provided by a standard Regency Small Stateroom (complete with amenities).
