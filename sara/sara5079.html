Tokugawa Destroyer

design by Peter Gray

While I was hunting through my old design papers, I found this little ship almost virtually complete. I remember that I had intended it for Imperial service, but its name seems to evoke more of the old Solomani Confed Navy. If the armor seems to be a little strange, its because, like many of my older designs, it was based upon a HG style hull percentage.

The Tokugawa class Destroyer was the standard medium sized escort ship utilised by the old Solomani Confederacy Navy. First constructed in 1092 by a consortium of companies in the Near Bootes Cluster, it quickly became a workhorse for many Solomani DesRons. While of fairly conventional construction, it was far more durable than many of its Imperial contemporaries, and combined a hefty 12 GJ N-PAW with robust laser and missle armament, and a squadron of eight Paarma 20 ton fighters for screening and reconaissance. Accomodation space is based upon S&A, which permits a 20% cut to reflect the Solomani indifference to crowding.
