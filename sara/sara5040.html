Aries Patrol Cruiser

design by Derek Stanley

Originally layed down in 1184 the Aries-Class Patrol Cruiser was originally designed as a single use system assault boat, designed with one purpose and one purpose only. To each bring 200 Marines from Stonehaven's surface to the K'pinah Orbital Starport, the Marines were to assualt the station, seize control and defend it from any viral assault. The operation was a complete disaster, the station was lost and two of the original four Aries-Class Assault Ships were lost in the ensuing blood bath, one to friendly fire.

The remaining Aries were quickly redesigned and out fitted for system defence duties. This refit included the removal of the troop bays and replacing them with a pair of short range Particle Accelerators and the addition of a midline mounted collapsable spin habitat. Four more Aries were hastilly constructed with these specifications, three of which were lost securing the system.

By 1189 the people of Stonehaven were ready to secure the remainder of the Stonehaven Cluster, a grouping of three stars barely inside Reavers Deep. The Aries were quickly refitted with J-1 Jump Drive's at the expence of manouver fuel. The Aries-Class Patrol Cruisers were born and in 1189 six of them jumped into the neighbouring Aries system, for whom they were named, and quickly established space superiority over a small number of system bound Vampires.

The Aries would eventually be outfitted with a larger Fission power plant and HEPlaR manouver drives before finally reaching the end of their useful life in 1195.
