Fury Battle Rider

design by Chris Thompson

The Fury is part of the complement of a Nightlord Battle Tender. The design was first though of during the years just prior to the Rebellion and construct took place before the start of the Rebellion though due to the Rebellion causing disruptions it wasn't finished until just before the Virus struck. The builders were the thrid/last Imperium and it was destined for the Imperial navy, the lack of nuclear dampers is due to the designer thinking that the armor would stop most hits or that the missiles would be shot down before reaching the vessel. There are a couple of house rules which governed these decisions, these are that any laser turret can engage a missile if with in range with a +2 difficulty modifier and unrefined fuel causes increased maintenance (build up of material in pipes etc.). Which is why the refining plant is there. The large number of communicators is to provide battle redundancy and allow data links/voice comms to be run to most of the ships in the squadron at once. The idea is all ships in the squadron pass data to tender and a flag staff located there coordinates the battle. The sensor are also backed up to off set battle damage as are the computers and most of the electronics. The onboard fighter squadron has it own fighter controller as part of the command crew of the rider.
