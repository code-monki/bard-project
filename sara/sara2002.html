Gofer Class Fuel/Cargo Shuttle

design by Franklin W. Cain

The Gofer shuttle is normally carried by a Tycoon Trader, but other modular ships such as the Aurora clipper series can carry them.


Gofer Class Cargo/Fuel Shuttle

General Data

Displacement: 50 tons Hull Armor: 14
Length: 14 meters Volume: 700 m3
Price: 18.084 MCr Target Size: S
Configuration: Box SL Tech Level: 12/11
Mass (Loaded/Unloaded): 641.586/218.086


Engineering Data

Power Plant: TL-11/12 50 MW Fusion plant (3.046 MW surplus; 50 MW/hit), one year duration
Jump Performance: 0
Fuel Tankage: 50 m3 and 7 m3 reserved for power plant
G-Rating: 1 G/HEPlaR (25 MW); Contra-Grav Lifters (5 MW)
G-Turns: 16 (3.125 m^3 each)
Maint: 29

Electronics

Computer: 2x TL-12 Model Standard (0.4 MW each)
Commo: 1x TL-12 30,000 km Radio (1 hx; 1 MW)
Avionics: TL-12 Flight Avionics
Sensors: 1x TL-12 30,000 km Active EMS (1 hx; 11 MW)
ECM/ECCM: none
Controls: Flight Deck with 1 workstation

Armament

Offensive 1 Turret Socket (Loc: 10; Arcs: 2, 3, 4)
Defensive: None
Master Fire Directors: None

Accommodations

Life Support: Extended (0.14 M)
Grav Compensation: 3 Gs (3.5 MW)
Crew: 1 Pilot
Crew/Passenger Accommodations: 1x Small Stateroom (0.0005 MW) 2x Low Berths (0.001 MW each) 4x Adequate Seats
Other Facilities: None
Cargo: 420 m3 2 Large Cargo Hatches
Small Craft and Launch Facilities: None
Air Locks: 1

Notes

  • When docked with a _Tycoon_ Modular Trader, the Shuttle provides the 25 MW needed by the _Tycoon_ for the maneuver drive's increased power usage.
  • Whenever a laser turret is installed, it is customary to plan for "overpowering" the weapon, as this is easily achieved whenever the Shuttle is docked with a _Tycoon_ Modular Trader. (The Radio, Active EMS, and CG Lifters on the Shuttle are unneeded and thus powered down, freeing a total of 17 MW with which to "overpower" the laser weapon.)
