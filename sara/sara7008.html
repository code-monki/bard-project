Goshawk Far Trader

design by Lewis Roberts

The Goshawk is an early version of the ubiquitous Far Trader. It was originally designed near the dawn of the Third Imperium, but did not meet with popular approval. It was only when the ship was redesigned as the Jayhawk, that the ship became somewhat popular, but by that time, the Empress Marva class was available, and the Jayhawk/Goshawk was regulated to a second class nature. The Goshawk and Jayhawk are identical in peformance, it is only in outward appearence that they differ. The Goshawk is rather more bulky looking, with protruding tail fins, which serve no other purpose, than to make the ship look exceedinly silly.

The ship is rather maintence hungry, while in jump space, much of the crew's tim e is spent in upkeep of the ship. The standard configuration of the ship is to have one sandcaster, but this can be switched for another weapon if desired. The electronics officer controls the sandcaster, but if a weapon is installed, a gu nnery officer should be hired.

The ship was designed using SSDS as presented in Starships.
