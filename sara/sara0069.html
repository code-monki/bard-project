Stafire Light Fighter

design by Antony Farrell

The F17 Stafire class light fighter was designed at the same time as the F16 Scimitar class light fighter, with which it shares many components. Not as fast as the Scimitars, having a maneuver drive capable of 4G compared to the Scimitars 5G, the Stafire does carry a somewhat heavier armament in the form of two 50-Mj Laser Lances compared to the Scimitars single.
