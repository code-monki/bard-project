Waorlasa�Eakhko Troop Transport

design by Yves Simon

The Waorlasa�Eakhko-Class troop transport evolved from an old Aslan design for Colony ships. Named after Aslan clan heads (from the few clans that settled in the Confederation), those are large ship capable of transporting in good condition up to 600 Grav tanks & APCs and 4600 troops (9200 in cramped conditions) and to drop them quite safely on any planetary surfaces. This can be done due the the secondary ships that are attached to the Waorlasa�Eakhko hull: 8 Assault Landers each 4000 dTons and capable of landing up to 30 12dTons Grav APCs and 300 troops.

It's cavernous hangars can hold easily 300 grav APCs (the remainding is serviced in the subcrafts) and the cargo hold can keep with the logistic of the troops. Capable of J-4 and 4-G Maneuver, it can keep with any average fleet. Capable of refueling at any Gas Giant also help reduce the burden of this ship to the fleet.

The major punch of the ship comes from 10 750Mj Laser lances located on the prow of the ship. The rest is quite light (150 300 Mj Laser Barbettes and 25 Missiles Barbettes). The defense is good with 30cm of BSD armor plating, 50 Sandcaster Turrets, 50 PDS Laser Turrets (5 arrays per turrets) and 25 Nuclear Damper Turrets. To survive any meson artillery on the target planet, a Meson screen is installed (1500 AV).

The Waorlasa�Eakhko can provide cover for it daughter craft landing with 12 70dT Heavy Fighters and 40 20dT light fighters. It is also fitted to launch 1 man drop capsules (generally around 100 are present). The ship operate in tandem as they can hold a full battalion splitted between each other.

The accommodations allows to house and entertain half a MarCav battalion. With the equivalent of 1000 LSR allocated for recreationnal areas, the troops can keep trained even after long trips (Several rooms for weapon training are present, and a small factory can build ammunition is raw materials are provided). A thousand troops are provided for replacement.
