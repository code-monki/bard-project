Shaar Muzaandi Heavy Transport

design by Peter Gray

This is a ship that was briefly mentioned in MTJ 2. It is pictured in V&V on Page 35, looking all the world like a big brother of the "Fat Trader." And like its smaller relative, the Shaar Muzaandi is intended to work J-1 mains, wending its way between closely clustered worlds cheaply and efficiently. Oddly, In the Regency the ship is more commonly encountered on the smaller mains in Deneb and Trojan Reach/Reft, than on the bigger Spinward Main in the Marches. Since the Collapse, the ship has been licensed to a number of Zhodani, Vargr and Spinward Extents manufacturers, making at least semi-abundant across the Spinward Safe. The crew for the ship's boat is drawn from other sections, usually the stewards and engineering crew. If weaponry is fitted, care must be made as the ship only possesses a small power surplus, requiring balancing between weaponry and other critical systems.
