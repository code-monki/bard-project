Mosquito Light Escort

design by Idiot/Savant

    "The Mosquito was designed to meet the standard Imperial Navy specification of 4G maneuver and jump-4 capability. Several squadrons of this class served with distinction throughout the Fourth Frontier War, and subsequent constrction on further models of this class was undertaken at the Rhylanor shipyards.

    "One of the most popular configurations in the latter stages of that war (and throughout the Fifth Frontier War) was as a quasi-carrier for fighters and landers. A typical configuration saw the pinnace replaced by a Type 1072 lander and the 104-ton cargo hold loaded with two lance-class heavy fighters. The resulting offensive punch and operational versatility caused the Imperial Design Review Board to belatedly designate the Mosquito as an IDP design in 1111. This configuration is now quite popular with Black War strike groups, particularly those which anticipate a need for operational flexability and/or fighter screens."

    -- "Ships of the Black War", Challenge #60, p 29.

As presented, this was a J4 G4 1000 DspT escort, armed with 6 missile and 4 sand turrets, and with 104 DspT of cargo space. The converted version maintains the original socket layout, but substitutes 4 300 Mj laser barbettes for some of the missiles. Cargo space is reduced to 82 DspT, providing minimal hanger space for one craft, or alternatively docking ring space for two. A minimal hanger is also provided for a 40 DspT utility vehicle, usually a pinnace.

Possible modifications: put the Pinnace in a docking ring, rather than a hanger - but does this allow other craft to be carried?
