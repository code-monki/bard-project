Marco Polo Colony Ship

design by Eamon Watters

The Marco Polo Class Colonizer Ship that the L'Steichians used to support the Colonization of Heppling (Baldur). The Hellenic Transport and Trading Corporation (HTTC) owns two of these, which with the Low Berths removed, provide outsize cargo transport in the RC. It's well defended for two reasons - L'Steichian politics could get heated at times, and so it was deemed prudent to arm the vessels. Also, when not in use shuttling Colonists to Heppling or Bestor, the owners would farm it out to other governments in the the area, usually Clallum. IIRC there was also a large pirate threat pre-collapse, High Passage detailed well armed pirates operating out of Sarid (Oriflamme) Subsector. Supported by the Solomani, they were well trained and well armed.
