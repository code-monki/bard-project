Vilardi Armoured Cruiser

design by Antony Farrell

The Vilardi class armoured cruiser was designed to meet the Third Imperiums type CA-11 specifications. Not a front-line cruiser they enjoyed some success when used in lower threat areas. They finally came into their own towards the end of the Rebellion when most of the higher tech vessels had been lost. The design itself is probably of Terran rather than Vilani origin given the very cramped crew accomodation and heavy armament.
