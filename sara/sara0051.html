TL-15 Military Shuttle

design by Name of designer

This ship is an upgraded version of the civilian 95 dT shuttle. It is fitted with better communication & sensor systems. It is also fitted with some ECM/ Stealth capabilities.
It's offensive capability consiste in a single 150 Mj Laser Turret.

The main mission of this ship is to transport crew/cargo from/to a ship. This ship is also capable of skimming a gas giant, but cannot process fuel.
