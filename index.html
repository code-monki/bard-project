BARD is the Reformation Coalition's compilation of library data. BARD's primary task is to facilitate tourism and trade as well as to provide general information to Coalition citizens. The BARD system currently contains only a limited amount of information. Much of the pre-Collapse Imperial information was found to be incorrect and must be re-examined before being released. As new reports are developed, Director Philibus will announce the addition on COALINFONET.
