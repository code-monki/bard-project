Rusco
D64669C-6

by Eamon Watters

This world was an important producer of merchant vessels before the collapse, but it was hit hard by a Solomani strike during the Black War, destroying the starport and it's surrounds. The VTA has attempted to recover jump drive production machinery from the world, but has only retrieved old field re-alignment equipment. This is currently being installed at Dycarl's starport, and will enable it to carry out quicker drive maintenance. The planet exports agricultural products, pharmaceuticals, and Droyne goods. There are a large amount of Droyne Technicians from this world working at the Starport and Scout Base on Voskl, where they are performing apparent miracles refurbishing old starships. The Starport was almost completely built by Voskl.
