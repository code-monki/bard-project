Karabeth

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class E
Diameter: 5667 Miles
Atmosphere: Standard
Surface Water: 35%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 95,500
Government: Subjugated (Liiri)
Law Level: 8
Tech Level: 7

Liiri's other colony has seen a major population boom since the Collapse. While not the most comfortable of worlds, it possesses a startlingly beautiful body of water at its North Pole known as the Crystal Sea. Even during the summer, local temperatures remain moderate enough to enjoy the longer periods of daylight. A local form of zooplankton native to the sea filters the water to a high level of purity, to the point that it will be contaminated if held in a nominally clean glass container. The tourist trade provide most of the worlds seasonal income, even though the locals don't completely welcome it.
