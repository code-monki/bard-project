Bwan Murr
UPP: X455778-5

by Lewis Roberts

Bwan Murr has no working spacecraft but does have a working albiet primitive planetary defense system, which consists of planetary radar sites and ballistic missles. The government is a run by the Reverend Desmond who preaches that the stars are populated by devils and offworlders are their servants. The government treats its populace pretty well, it just attempts to kill strangers on sight.

References: Death of Wisdom, Path of Tears
