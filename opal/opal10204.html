Subsector Notes

Srys subsector has a New Era population of just over 8.15 billion, a decline of over 56.5 billion from its pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 2 billion, at Srys, and its highest tech level is 8, at Exage and Sarcathon. Three worlds have actually experienced a population increase over their pre-Collapse levels: Ranb, Sarcathon, and Zinorow. The Droyne on Yeroltoy have eliminated the small human population that was on that world.
