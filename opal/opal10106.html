Forquee Subsector: Forquee Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 16.8 billion, a decline of 85.8percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 10 billion at Arlur, and its highest tech level is 11 at Forquee.

The Forquee Subsector (historical): Forquee Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 118 billion. Its highest population was 90 billion at Forquee, and its highest tech level was 15 at Wander and Gowandon.

Subsector Notes

Forquee (1719)

Liaw(2017)

P'ley (1818): This world, satellite of a gas giant, holds a colony of Forquee, established in 1190.

Udipeni (2316): Udipeni, famous in pre-Rebellion times for its Iilodav Music Festival, has experienced an 80% population decrease with respect to that times. The charismatic oligarchy which originally governed the world has evolved into four separate, fighting groups which hold power in their respective regions through control of relic technology.
