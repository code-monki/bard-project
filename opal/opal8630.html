Mushinag

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class D, Regency Scout Base
Diameter: 4875 Miles
Atmosphere: Standard with Sulfur taint
Surface Water: 46%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 4.0 Million
Government: Technocracy
Law Level: 6
Tech Level: 9

Mushinag has an axial tilt of 84 degrees, with its poles facing its primary. Aside from some very dramatic weather shifts the world is otherwise unremarkable.
