Lan-chou

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class D, Regency Scout Base, Trade Terminus
Diameter: 7784 Miles
Atmosphere: Thin
Surface Water: 51%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 6.43 Million
Government: Civil Service Bureaucracy
Law Level: 8
Tech Level: 7

Lan-chou was settled around 388, and its population grew in response for demand in foodstuffs on nearby Giikusu. The world lost a significant amount of its population to the Abandonment, and local industry and agriculture have had to realign to compensate. Previously its primary products were meat and hides from its extensive ranches, which cultivated a local animal known as the Shlurry, a cow sized animal that vaguely resembles the Terran potato bug. But the ranching industry was hurt by flagging demand and depopulation, and more and more ranchers have abandoned the export business altogether. Most have gone into light industry and energy "farming", the latter being mostly solar and wind generators utilised to power factories that manufacture components and Pre-stellar technology for local use or export to nearby Giikusu. The local government has plans to modernise the world now that the Frontier has been lifted, and is eagerly seeking new immigrants on overcrowded worlds like Lilad or Deneb.
