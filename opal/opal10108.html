Ten Suns Subsector: Ten Suns Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 1.3 billion, a decline of 92.8 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 1 billion at Marach and its highest tech level is 8 at Bloom.

Ten Suns Subsector (historical): Ten Suns Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 18 billion. Its highest population was 8 billion at Dirac, and its highest tech level was 16 at Quuke and Huon.
