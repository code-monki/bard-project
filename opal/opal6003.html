Leenitakot

D77A6X4-4

by Eamon Watters
Site of the famous Leenitakot Ringworld, and inspiration for the naming of the Ring Pact, it's Droyne inhabitants are notoriously easy-going. Formerly a possession of the Outcasts of the Whispering Sky, it is now the site of the Ringworld Research Institute, a Pact division. Although incomplete, the massive structure has plenty of secrets and artefacts to surprise investigators, many of whom travel from the Federation to investigate it. The post collapse history of the world is quite similar to Yoryunssoy's, save for the initial battles against their oppressive Outcast overlords that wiped them from the face of the planet. It gets reasonable cross-trade from Gammist to the Strom Cluster and the far end of the Elan Main, and is more open about Psionic training then their brothers, but less accomplished.
