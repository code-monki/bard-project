Subsector Notes

Ahrhi subsector has a New Era population of just over 425 million, a decline of over 72.4 billion from its pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 100 million, at Algren, and its highest tech level is 8, at Drand. Two worlds have actually experienced a population increase over their pre-Collapse levels: Algren and Drand. The people of Louks have mangaged to survive at a level just below their pre-Collapse level in spite of an unusual atmospheric condition.
