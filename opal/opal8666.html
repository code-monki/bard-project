Khuaeakha

by Pete Gray

Starport:Class C
Diameter:6218 Miles
Atmosphere:Standard
Surface Water:91%
Climate:Very Cold
Population:8.39 Million (2.12 Million Aslan)
Government: Aslan Balkanised (12 different clans)
Law Level: 4
Tech Level: 7

Formerly known as Brufort. The world was conquered in 1124 by a clan allied with the Seieakh bloc, and the world was declared fait accompli in 1132. Because its new masters were abusive of the indigenous human population, the Regency government granted land to other friendly clans to counteract their influence. Several clan wars have occured since the Collapse between the original invaders and new arrivals, and the world is heavily militarised as a result. The integration of the Aslan has been complicated by the harsh local climate, which has foiled the long-term economic plans of several clans. Some clans have become radicalised by their hardships, and the wars have become more vicious in the last decade or so.
