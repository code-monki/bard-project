Javan

by Pete Gray

Starport:Class C, Regency Scout Base
Diameter:7237 Miles
Atmosphere:Standard with low moisture (ice desert)
Surface Water:97% water ice
Climate:Frozen
Population:378
Government: Technocracy (various government agencies)
Law Level: 3
Tech Level: 10

Frozen Javan never attracted any interest other than the Scouts. The base is a surveillance facility that moniters for Vampire Fleets moving out of the Rift. The local population are all base dependents or contractors that maintain the local sensor arrays.
