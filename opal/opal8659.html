Inkekush

by Pete Gray

Starport:Class A, Regency Naval Base
Diameter:1142 Miles
Atmosphere:Thin with heavy industrial pollution
Surface Water:None
Climate:Very Hot
Population:559,000
Government: Represenative Democracy
Law Level: 8
Tech Level: 15

Inkekush is one of the Spinward Complex Reserve worlds. It is the main support facility for operations in the complex, maintaining ships and manufacturing logistics. The world is a fully self contained TL-15 manufacturing center, and is rumored to have been equipped to survive a possible fall of the Regency to Virus or Vampire infection. Because of its military importance, the health of the local environment is a secondary consideration; heavy air and water pollution plagues the world, and the heavy brown haze produced by munition factories that cover the world is visible from space. To compensate most of the population lives in self-contained underground arcologies complete with their own farms and enough life support to run for years without outside support.
