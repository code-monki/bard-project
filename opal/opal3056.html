Linnert

D-642444-8

by Liam Devlin

Linnert is one of the Coreward systems in Jayna recontacted & re-opened using the Trade Co- prosperity technique by VADM Warner Castle (Pronounced Cas-lay) of the "Jayna Fleet" of Solee after the reclamation of Depot /Ultraneta in 1201. The Charismatic Dictator, Hugh Thingolis saw the immediate advantages of being kept in his position for life, and recognized by a Stellar polity the size of the "Soleean Empire".

Linnert is used as a rest world for the crews & workers Solee-Galines-Czerniak have at Ultraneta Jump-2 to trailing. Not to mention having them spend their pay here, Linnert's defense force militia has received upgraded weaponry to TL-B, and looks forward to the rebuilding of their shipyard with enthusiasm. As of 001-1202 starport is D-class. Most, if not all of Linnert's inhabitants are employed here, or in the surrounding countryside. Linnert is considered a gas Giant moon, the sole satellite of the SGG in the 0 orbit of the system M1 V star.

Armed forces

  • SAD: 250, Starport crews TL-8 controls/TL-B PADM's consisting of 1 battery, with four launchers. Experienced crews

  • Ground Forces: 500x are employed as "Security Forces"(the local Police), at TL-8. Into five precincts of 100 personnel each

  • ACC: 250 personnel, maintaining & manning 2x TL-8 VTOL Harrier sub-orbital interceptors.
