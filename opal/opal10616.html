Corridor

Manifest Destiny Campaign

    Chris Griffen created a campaign setting set in 1212 in Corridor sector. In it, colonists from the Regency have established several fledgling polities. There is the conflict between the Regency which wants to exert greater control over the colonies, while the colonies struggle to remain free from both the Regency and the Megacorporations. There is the everpresent danger of Virus hanging over everyone's head. The files are in PDF, and are fairly large.
