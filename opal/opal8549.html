Jurburk

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class C, Regency Scout Base, Trade Terminus
Diameter: 3991 Miles
Atmosphere: Thin
Surface Water: 42%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 347
Government: Technocracy
Law Level: 3
Tech Level: 10

In Antebellum times, Jurburk was a backwater, a whistle stop for merchant ships moving down the Midway to more settled areas of the Domain of Deneb. The original B-class starport was maintained for cheap refuelling, but the world itself was largely ignored. After the Collapse its remote location and scout base put it into the Regency Frontier as an important sentry against Vampire incursions. With the opening of the Regency Frontier, the world has become one of several designated as Quarantine terminals for trade and passengers moving into and out of the Wilds. The above listed population are mostly transient construction workers building a new starport expansion.
