Tooka Subsector: Tooka Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 1.2 billion, a decline of 89.3 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 1 billion at Giirsha, and its highest tech level is 9 at Haela.

Tooka Subsector (historical): Tooka Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 11.2 billion. Its highest population was 10 billion at Giirsha, and its highest tech level was 16 at Tooka and Glerum.
