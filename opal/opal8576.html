Cabrini

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class C, Regency Scout Base
Diameter: 3009 Miles
Atmosphere: Standard
Surface Water: 51%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 304,100
Government: Corporate (Ling Standard)
Law Level: 0
Tech Level: 7

Cabrini is the site of several major Ling Standard production plants. Mostly these are chemical and mine processing units that extract raw materials from the highly saline oceans. LSP obtained the local scout base as a means of providing additional income for the starport. The world's commercial value has declined since the Civil War and Collapse, and the factories were mostly shuttered. The majority of its money is now made through the port, as traffic transits from Sabinar Main to the Star Lane and Spinward Marches. The local law level permits nearly every form of traffic and commerce possible, a practice encouraged by the company managers, and scrutinised by large RQS patrols.
