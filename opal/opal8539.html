Haggard

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class D, Regency Scout Base
Diameter: 6558 Miles
Atmosphere: Standard with heavy pollution
Surface Water: 72%
Climate: Chilly
Population: 327 plus transients
Government: Participatory Democracy
Law Level: 4
Tech Level: 5

Haggard is a de facto part of the Regency by virtue of the high security scout base located near the downport. Like Errogel's naval base, this facility is primarily for intelligence gathering and salvage. Most of the population are base dependents and a few brave souls that refused evacuation when the world was first abandoned. The base is located entirely underground, and is a level 4 containment facility that was originally constructed for the Scout Exploratory Office. Free traders peddling scrap and useful salvage are a common sight on this world, particularly members of The Souk Free Trader Collective.
