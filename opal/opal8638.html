Alaungpaya

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class C, Regency Scout Base
Diameter: 693 Miles
Atmosphere: Thin tainted by industrial wastes
Surface Water: None
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 95.8 Million
Government: Impersonal Bureaucracy
Law Level: 4
Tech Level: 10

Alaungpaya is little more than a large, nickel-iron planetoid with an atmosphere. The rock's high density has permitted it to keep its atmosphere from leaking completely into space. In the antebellum period it was an industrial center of minor importance, producing Pre-Stellar goods for export to worlds in Usani and Zeng subsectors. Factories belched out pollution that overwhelmed the world's tenuous environment, and since the Collapse the local government has distanced itself from heavy industry and searched for economic alternatives.
