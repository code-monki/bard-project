Shimmer Subsector:Shimmer Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 1.25 billion, a decline of 87.1 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 600 million at Devan, and its highest tech level is 9 at Luri.

The Shimmer Subsector (historical): Shimmer Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 10.5 billion. Its highest population was 9 billion at Faro, and its highest tech level was 14 at Todkar, Binsarah and Virim.
