Nes'vra: Nes'vra Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 640 million, a decline of 96.4 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 600 million at Muirmarir, and its highest tech level is 8 at Bern.

The Nes'vra Subsector (historical): Nes'vra Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 18 billion. Its highest population was 9 billion at Muirmarir, and its highest tech level was 15 Bern.
