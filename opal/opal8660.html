Saguenay

by Pete Gray

Starport:Class B, Regency Naval Base
Diameter:3526 Miles
Atmosphere:Very Thin
Surface Water:78%
Climate:Warm
Population:646
Government: Technocracy
Law Level: 6
Tech Level: 15

Saguenay is another member of the Spinward Complex, in this case an instrumented naval test range and proving grounds. Ships from the 303rd and the 2nd Experimental Fleets often use the world's northern continent for gunnery practice, and the region is interdicted for safety and security reasons. The listed population is mostly civilian support personnel, or their dependents, that operate and maintain the local facilities. The downport is located near the world's south pole as a protective measure against errant ordinance.
