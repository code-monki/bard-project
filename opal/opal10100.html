Kerr Subsector: Kerr Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 37 billion, a decline of 74.5% percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 30 billion at Kaggushus, and its highest tech level is 12 at Towering and Kaggushus.

Kerr Subsector (historical): Kerr Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 145 billion. Its highest population was 90 billion at Kaggushus, and its highest tech level was 16 at Ticularosta and Kaggushus.

Subsector Notes

Several worlds are written up in the Traveller: The New Era rulebook on page 175-176.
