Shokee Subsector: Shokee Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 6.2 billion, a decline of 95.2 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 5 billion at Nagiida, and its highest tech level is 15 at Jade.

The Shokee Subsector (historical): Shokee Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 130 billion. Its highest population was 80 billion at Nagiida, and its highest tech level was 15 at Jade and Nobody.

Subsector Notes

Llyrn (0931)

Nagiida (1233)

Garency (1133)

The New Era population of Shokee subsector is 7,300 million. Highest tech level is F at Jade, a world controlled by Virus. Ashavakuna belongs to the Geonee (a minor human race) Pocket Empire centered in Shiwonee subsector. Llyrn is the homeworld of a minor alien race.. Tanny, Nilton and Wewarbinner were colonized by a corporation transporting fleeing refugees from worlds damaged by the war during Hard Times, and now they form a small Pocket Empire known as The Tanny Project.

Ashavakuna: See Shiwonee subsector notes

Jacat (0933): This world is the home base for the Blue Pearl corsair band, which preys mainly on starships from the nearby Geonee Confederation and from Nagiida. The flagship of the fleet is the Triple Fear, a still operative patrol cruiser from Rebellion times.

Jade (1634): In this system, a Virus 4 strain (Puppeteer) controls a small fleet of non-jump capable spaceships, constantly fighting and re-infecting each other. Part of the spaceships are damaged starships from Rebellion times, and others have actually been constructed in the Virus-controlled, fully automated starport.

Weemar (1234): Mining colony from the Dekaan Republic of Nagiida.

Scole (1335): Colony set up by Zunarsha Corporation, from Nagiida. In practice, Zunarsha Scole is a subsidiary of the main company which operates with a fair degree of autonomy.
