Palasha Subsector: Palasha Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 3.7 billion, a decline of 86.8 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 2 billion at Daiid, and its highest tech level is 9 at Porto.

The Palasha (historical): Palasha Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 28 billion. Its highest population was 10 billion at Porto, and its highest tech level was 16 at Porto.
