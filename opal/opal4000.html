Mitchell's Vampire Fleet

X100000-0

by Derek Stanley
Here are the elements of the Vampire fleet stranded at Mitchell in Khulam Subsector in Diaspora. I felt that if there were ships in there in excess of 1000dt's the would likely have been mentioned in Path of Tears. I didn't bother making detailed personalities on these ships but did do a quick card sellection and a couple of them sounded pretty interesting..

      Name: Bunker C.
      Strain: Explorer
      Motivation: Stubborn
  • Monitor Class Scout (TL15 laser turret)
      Name: Nebula
      Strain: Explorer
      Motivation: Selfish

  • Gazelle Class Close Escort (2xTL14 laser turret, 1xTL14 laser barbette)
