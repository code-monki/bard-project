Vaait Subsector: Vaait Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 10.7 billion, a decline of 64.3 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 10 billion at Epel, and its highest tech level is 7 at Dalken and Neoland.

The Vaait Subsector (historical): Vaait Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 30 billion. Its highest population was 20 billion at Aarze, and its highest tech level was 16 at Shimshan.

Subsector Notes

Lysitihia (1012): This hospitable world was a vacation location for rich business men. The planet was held by a company which had agreement with the Imperium, to handle the planet. The installationconsists mainly of Hotels and entertainment facilities. The ground starport was very luxuryous for the number of people living on this planet.

When the virus struck, The starport exploded due to hydrogen release. As the Starport was quite far from the city (200Km) there weren't many casualities. Most of the people left to buildings for a while. Actually, the population was divided by 5, not because of the Virus but because of lack of surviving skill. This little remnant of society tried to organized itself. Half of them lives in the hotels, the others are spread in the country side. The people have several cars and very few small weapons.
