Isurkun

by Pete Gray

Starport: Class A
Diameter: 3816 Miles
Atmosphere: Thin tainted with pollutants
Surface Water: 7%
Climate: Very Hot
Population: 9770
Government: Oligarchy
Law Level: 7
Tech Level: 13

Isurkun was the site of major antebellum industrial investment by the Vilani megacorporation Naasirka. The huge assembly plants it constructed were abandoned in 1121, and remain vacant and rotting to this day, and are referred to as the "elephant graveyard." The major unemployment caused by this pullout nearly caused the world to be abandoned, but the local oligarchy secured enough offworld investment to create new enterprises, and Isurkun prospers once again. The association with the Vilani led to a strong emphasis upon education, and Isurkun has the highest percentage of college degrees of any world in the Regency. The locals run a number of small liberal arts schools that are in high demand for training of teachers and university instructors.
