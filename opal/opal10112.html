Annari subsector: Annari Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 3.5 billion, a decline of 95.5 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 2 billion at Orichalc, and its highest tech level is 9 at Orichalc and Sorrento.

The Annari Subsector (historical): Annari Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 78 billion. Its highest population was 70 billion at Leam, and its highest tech level was 16 at Lowisa and Forever.
