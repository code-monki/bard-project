Wescom Subsector: Wescom Subsector has an estimated population in 1202 of approximately 266 million, a decline of 99.8 percent from its Pre-Collapse population. Its highest population is 200 million at Misthaven, and its highest tech level is 7 at Jenina.

The Wescom Subsector (historical): Wescom Subsector had an estimated population in 1117 of approximately 86 billion. Its highest population was 70 billion at Ortega, and its highest tech level was 16 at Cordell and Vonda.
