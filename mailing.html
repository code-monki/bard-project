Much of the material on the BARD pages is from the TNE-RCES mailing list. The list is dedicated to talking about Traveller: the New Era in all its forms, not just the Reformation Coalition. Its a small list of people who love to talk about Traveller .

To join:

    To subscribe, send 'subscribe TNE-RCES' as the body of a message to tne-rces-subscribe@silent-tower.org. For help, send the word 'help'.
