Victory/Defeat Conditions

Victory Points
    -10 for each ship lost and not recaptured.
    -2for each Dawn League/RC prisoner lost and not recaptured. (Note: There are 18 prisoners total.)
    +20 for each hostile starship destroyed or captured (other than Clipper; "hostile" means the ship attacked Keipes *first*)
    +30 for destroying Clipper.

Automatic Defeat Any one of the following:
    Losing *both* jump-capable vessels (Liner and Free Trader).
    Losing *all* prisoners.

Overwhelming Victory Condition Having the highest VP total

Marginal Victory Condition Having the second-highest VP total.
