Black Dawn LLC.

by Peter Gray

Black Dawn LRC is a customer focused, interstellar enterprise principally engaged in the research, design, development, manufacture and integration of advanced technology systems, products, and services for government and commercial customers. Black Dawn LRC operates in the same context as its customers and is deeply involved in solving the problems and meeting the demands and expectations of its customers. Moreover, it leads the way in showing customers new systems-based possibilities and opportunities.

The Corporation's core business areas are systems integration, aeronautics, space, and technology services. Black Dawn's vision is to be a leading research and development incubator in aerospace, defense and technology services; to be the company our nation and its allies trust most to integrate their largest, most complex, most important advanced technology systems. Our goal is to provide the best value to our customers, growth opportunities to our employees, and superior returns to our stockholders.

Black Dawn LRC had 1201 sales of 240.2 Gigacredits and employs approximately 35,000 people. Our main offices are located on Karst/Namidshur, but we have labs and yards located across the Regency, and consulting and coordination facilities located in several of the Regency's allies and neighbors.

Ever since the company was founded, it has been commited to a strong ethical course. We value our reputation, our customers and shareholders trust, and the character and integrity of our employees, and see all of these as a part of a greater vision that assimilates corporate responsibility with corporate profitability.

Black Dawn LRC's symbol is of an Imperial Sunburst, with a superimposed image of a shadowy raven. It is a double symbol. The raven is a warning against self-righteous sanctimony, but it is also a sign of regeneration, for a bird surround by the sun is also the Chinese phoenix.
