    NOTES:

    1 - All ships are registered by a palnet of the RC. This planet collects a per ton tax on all goods transported by the ship, and in return provides a certification of safety for the ship on an anual basis. The planet of registry determines the law codes onboard the ship when it is not docked. See also Registry.
    2 - der Krieger means Warrior in German.
    3 - Zauberer means Wizard in German.
    4 - Skakac is the Serbo-Croatian name for the Knight Chess Piece.
    5 - Diablotin Angelique is French for Angelic Imp.
    6These five ships were bought by Transstar from Free Traders. Each was refurbished at the Aurora Shipyard.
    7 - These three ships are currently under construction.
