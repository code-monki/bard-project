Ring Pact

by Eamon Watters
Kandra subsector of Hinterworlds is home to the Ring Pact, a Defence and Trade organisation sponsored by the Hive Federation. Each member has it's own needs and plans, but despite these centrifugal forces the Pact has held over the ten years of it's existence. Member worlds and possession of these worlds are indicate by a RP in the allegiance column.

Worlds of the Pact

Strom(3102)

Strom controls the following systems:

    Exit(97??). This cemetery world is controlled by the military, and contains a Hiver Staging Base.

    Vizier(97??). This cemetery world has a frontier starport to support trade along the Elan Main.

    Slider(3102)

    Dodo(97??). Formerly known as Elan, this thin-atmosphered world perished in the collapse. It's numerous relics provided a boon to Strom, and to a lesser extent her neighbours. There are still recovery missions underway here, but the tempo is much less than when the Tech Rush started in 1147. this world has always been a sore point with Strom's neighbours, and even though it has been opened to outsiders, who need Dodo's TL-A equipment less and less, it still rankles.

Yoryunssoy(3102)

Leenitakot(97??) Site of the famous Leenitakot Ringworld, and inspiration for the naming of the Ring Pact, it's Droyne inhabitants are notoriously easy-going. Formerly a possession of the Outcasts of the Whispering Sky, it is now the site of the Ringworld Research Institute, a Pact division. Although incomplete, the massive structure has plenty of secrets and artefacts to surprise investigators, many of whom travel from the Federation to investigate it. The post collapse history of the world is quite similar to Yoryunssoy's, save for the initial battles against their oppressive Outcast overlords that wiped them from the face of the planet. It gets reasonable cross-trade from Gammist to the Strom Cluster and the far end of the Elan Main, and is more open about Psionic training then their brothers, but less accomplished. Gammist(3102)

Gamvina's colonies are:

    (1)Sharina(97??). The only reason to visit this cemetery world is to use the Scout Base located here. The Gamvina government hopes to use it to gain riches recovering relics from the worlds of the Pusan-Bruia main. There have been some notable successes, but recently ships have been disappearing in the rimward section of the Main.

    (2)Nerkaal(97??). This world is a link in the Jump-1 trade through the Elan Main.

Driank(97??) A cemetery world, this system is only important because of the Ring Pact Naval Base located here. A squadron of Hiver and Pact ships make sweeps of the surrounding space from here. Many people consider it the best achievement of the Pact so far.
