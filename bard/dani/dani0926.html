Wallace Estralla
Age 25 in 1201

by Hailey Yerence

    Occupation: Unemployed Starship Pilot
    Attributes: 687789-7
    Background:
      The Estralla family (probably not the original family name) was lucky. They managed to keep their Jayhawk freighter, Sylvan Dancer, when most other ships were infected with Virus or destroyed by it. Since the collapse they have been trading with anyone with goods. The crew are the descendants of the original crew.

      Wallace never knew any other home. It was always one planet to the next, sometimes an inhabited planet sometimes not. At times it could be boring, but it gave him a chance that few kids had: to see the galaxy. As the son of the captain's line, Wallace became an officer almost instantly. He had no formal education, receiving his training as an apprentice would; on the job. Mathematics came naturally to him so he found astrogation especially appealing.

      Just before Wallace turned twenty, the Sylvan Dancer went on a long mission into the wilds after a reported cache of relic technology. The trip was a disaster. The planet did have relics, which were controlled by a paranoid TED. The crew only found this out, however, after they landed. Three members of the crew where killed in the escape from the planet and the sort space battle with the planet's decrepit systems defence boat. Fuel was short,but Wallace charted a coarse that took them directly to the next systems gas giant. Wallace's parents, who had been captain and first officer had been killed, since they usually led the trading efforts. His older sister, Vallan, the oldest in the captain's line, took over. As second oldest Wallace became first officer.

      The Sylvan Dancer made the long trip back to the area of the Reform Coalition, where it did much of its trading. For the couple years things were fairly quiet. It occurred to Wallace that the Sylvan Dancer was becoming a little crowded. His younger brother Xander had taken a wife, and the engineer had just had another child. Keeping the population of the ship low enough had always been a problem, since there were often more kids then parents. Wallace began to consider other options.

      The final factor in his decision was Vallan's announcement that her boyfriend, a dirtside trader, had proposed to her. Wallace knew Val would not leave her ship, which meant her husband would come to live on the Sylvan Dancer. By tradition, when the captain's spouse gained enough experience (s)he became first officer. Knowing he would lose his spot as first officer and realizing the ship was getting overcrowded anyway, Wallace decided to start making inquiries about jobs on other ship. With experience as a pilot and an astrographer in addition to a trader, and a talent for dealing with people, Wallace felt confident he would be able to find employment.

    Motivation:
      Heart Ace: Wallace has not had too much time to build a reputation, but what reputation he has is as an honest man who will offer a fair deal. Having been to many planets and encountering many cultures, he has few prejudices, one being cheats and liars. That has made finding a job more difficult, since he refuses to deal with the Mercantile Guild and some free traders.

      Heart 2: Wallace has an easy, open manner that has served him well in his excursions as a trader. He is pleasant, but also has a strong desire for privacy, something he did not have enough of as a child. He rarely talks about himself or his past beyond his qualifications, making him difficult to know well.
