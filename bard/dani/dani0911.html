    History:
      Nony is a undercover agent for the Fijan World Police. She recently resigned her post for unknown reasons. She has since been hired by Assemblyman MacBaen of Aurora to be part of his independent recovery effort. It has been postulatedt by unnamed sources that she is still working for the Fija government, but now spying against other worlds of the RC.
    Contacts:
      Maria Von Bek-You met Von Bek while working for the Fijan Intelligence branch of the Fijan World Police. The World Police are the one division of the government that is not up for bid. It acts independently except for a review panel, which investigates any police misconduct. The Intelligence branch investigates plots against the government and also attempts by Technocrats to influence the bidding process through illegal means. Von Bek was a top field agent. You served under her during the '95 Twin Skys Scandal, where you helped undercover a massive plot to rig the bidding process. After that you worked together occasionaly.

      Khamyannah Arsto-You met Khamyannah when you were investigating possible corruption in the Ministry of Development. She was an Assistant Commissioner and was made liason between the Ministry and the World Police. With her help you eventually cracked the case. You met her several times after that, usually at official functions. You heard that she has transfered to the Reformation Coalilition Service Administration and now heads up the Commision for External Development. It is the commision in charge of all bootstrap and colonization missions in the Wilds. Her office is located on the capital of the RC, Aubaine. She gets sick of being stuck behind a desk and often takes tours into the Wilds to inspect boostrap missions. For more information on Khamyannah Arsto, see Star Vikings.
