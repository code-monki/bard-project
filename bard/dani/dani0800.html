Miller Philibus
Age 41 in 1201

by Paul Walker

    Occupation:Director, BARD Library
    Attributes: 698A9A-7
    Background:
      Born on Vras on 136-1161, Miller is the son of very common parents. His father was a humble, quiet fisherman on Vras and his mother worked in a local factory. Miller was the third of five children and his parents barely made enough money to survive much less send him to college, so Miller joined the Vras Marines on a work school program. Miller served the Marines for four years and was quickly transferred to Intelligence. Miller became a liaison between the military and local police forces. Often he went undercover to help in sting operations against Humanist League Organizations on Vras.

      Miller's parents both died when he was on a mission in 1191. He has since drifted further and further from his siblings who found it difficult to forgive him for not being there. Miller was soon recalled by the military to work with the newly foroming interstellar community. Shortly after the Dawn League was established, Miller was sent on a mission to neighboring Phoebus, and after working 6 months undercover, Millier was injured in a fall during a mountain climbing trip. Miller was returned to Aubaine and given the proper medical treatment and he recovered.

      Miller's recovery was kept a secret, and he was made into a public hero by the Dawn league government and in 1198 he was given a Purple Heart and a Medal of Honor. Miller was assigned to head up the BARD Library, a public service offering Library information to Dawn League citizens. Behind the scenes, BARD was designed to be the primary clearing house for Dawn League intelligence reports.

    Appearance:
      Miller is slightly shorter than average, measuring in at 5'11". He is slim at 64kg. He has dark brown hair, cut very short, but not quite in a crew cut. He has blue eyes that always seem alive with life. Miller will usually be found wearing a casual outfit and when the weather calls for it, a Flight Jacket given to him by a close friend in the Navy. Although he owns several, Miller rarely wears an RCES body sleeve except when visiting the field. Miller has trained himself to limp when he walks even though the surgery was successful. Also as part of his 'immage' he will occasionally flinch or wince as though enduring a back spasm.
    Personality:
      Miller is a very funloving person. When he is not concentrating on his work with BARD, Miller will usually be found at parties. Although he recognizes the need for them, Miller detests 'official function of state' parties and generally despizes having to mill with the upper crust of society. Miller is much more comfortable in a corner bar with a group of friends.

      Millers first dedication is the BARD project. Although he will seldom admit it, Miller is often more inspired by the outward public library function of BARD than he is by the intelligence side. When he is working on the intelligence side, Miller is very devoted to the project. Miller firmly believes that the failure of the original 12 ships was due to a lack of proper intelligence gathering beforehand.

    Motivation:
      Diamond Ace: Miller is a generous person. He will often give to friends in need, but seldom gives money, as he would rather supply the item needed than impersonal money. He does get paid for his work, he has often tried to persuade the Assembly committe that he reports to that he doesn't want the money.
      Spade Nine: Miller is dedicated to the RC's success. He is very ambitious for the RC to succeed. Miller often has worked night and day when sorting information to prepare a RCES group for a certain mission.
    Refereeing Philibus:
      Quite simply, Miller is an information source and potential patron to the PC's. Miller is the go between for the Free Trader Network and other RCES Intelligence Operatives. Often Miller will be called on to present reports to PC's who are going out into the Wilds for a mission. He may have even designed the mission. Miller will be there for debriefings and will often offer to meet with the PC's for a drink or two after the debriefing.

      Miller also was given a Far Trader by the RC for his dedication and devotion. Although the ship has a standard crew, Miller might hire the PC's as freelancers to crew the ship while the other crew is taking vacation. The PC's might even get hired on as a new crew for Miller's ship. Most of the missions are for information gathering, but they have also proven to be quite profitable thus far.
