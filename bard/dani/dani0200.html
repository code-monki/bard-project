Elanor "Stormy" Treste
Age 34 in 1201

by Lewis Roberts

    Occupation: Force Commander, Oriflammen Marines
    Attributes: 876825-B
    Background:
      Treste was born on Oriflamme in 1168, to parents who worked for the newly formed Daishow Trust. They raised her to be dedicated to the Trust. When the Trust started to raise a unit for the Oriflamme Marines, she joined up. The Marines recognized her talent, and commissioned her as an officer. She specialized in counter-insurgency and guerrilla warfare. After the conquest of Spencer she was promoted and sent in to eliminate resistance. She has instituted draconian measures designed to eliminate support for the rebellion. Her efforts have proven effective, rebel activity has fallen by 45%.
