    History:
      Autumn was born on the planet Lucifer in 1178 to wealthy merchants. Her parents did not venture into space, mearly trading things across the planet. Autumn was brought up helping with the family business and intended to continue working with the company. This changed when Autumn met Capt Anne Bowlden of the free trader Whispering Dawn. Autumn immediately became infatuated with space and exploration. She bugged her parents until they allowed her to sign on with the Whispering Dawn. Her parents gave the Captain a profitable lot of furs and wools, as a bribe to take their daughter and to watch over her. She was a quick study and was made an officer within one year, and promoted after three years.

      The Whispering Dawn made many journeys into Alpha Crucis and the trailing portions of the Solomani Rim. Autumn became second in command of the ship after 9 years. Autumn began to see that the expanding Reformation Coalition needed experienced officers. She was proud to see her homeworld becoming part of something big. After a great deal of soul searching she decided to leave the Whispering Dawn and join the RCES. While her application was going through, she was contacted by Assemblyman MacBaen, and offered a job aboard the Argo. The mission was pretty much the same as the RCES and the pay was much better. So she signed up as the starboard gunner.
