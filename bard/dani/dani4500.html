David Keirsey Bates
Age 47 in 1201

by Alvin Plummer

    Occupation: Scout for the Ranger Corporation
    Attributes: ??????-?
    Background:
      The Ranger Corporation is a scout/exploration company, the largest now operating in the Spinward Marches. Currently has 20 scouts,and one scout tender. Based on Mora/Mora/Spinward Marches.

      His willingness to help, natural shyness and extreme skill in piloting endeared Bated to the crew of the Second Guess.

      While docked at one of Ruie's downports the crew decided to hit the bars, after trying unsuccessfuly to get Bates to join them. They never returned. The local police has not found a trace of them in two weeks of searching, and suspect that they have left the planet. Bates has contingency order to return to Mora, and is already looking for a crew to take the Second Guess home. However, he would love to make one last effort to find his shipmates.

      His family consists of a wife on his homeworld of Tirem/Glisten/Spinward Marches, five grown kids (one in the Regency Navy) According to his culture, he's to be refered to by his Anglic name (David Keirsey Bates)when outside of Tirem system, his Chinese (Wu San-kuei) name when insystem, and his Vilani name (Abama Shigadishuu) by the nobility (regardless of where he is)
