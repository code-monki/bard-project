Count Dietrich von Schunamann
Age 43 in 1200

by Alvin Plummer

    Occupation: CEO, Schunamann und Sohn, AG (the SuSAG megacorp)
    Attributes: 537467-E
    Background:
      von Schunamann is the achnowledged head of the Schunamann family. The 60 acknowledged family members form the core of SuSAG; once a megacorp with interest's across the Imperium, it's now restricted to the Regency providing chemicals, pharmaceuticals, biogeneered products, and semi-legal psi boosters.

      von Schunamann is only an adequate CEO, delegating much of his duties to trusted aides and pulling the regins only when he needs to. He spend's much of his time either meeting figurehead duties, enjoying himself in very high social circles or playing Mechwarrior with other profoundly wealthy men. [Mechawar is to the Regency what Polo was to the British Empire].

      von Schunamann is happly married - maintaining not even one concubine, to the astonishment of Court circles - and delight's in his eight children.

      Most of his families' treasured possessions is feared lost, somewhere in Magyar sector. The possessions that von Schunamann most loves, however, are his collection of TL F battle mecha. These 12-15 m high vechiles/androids, armed with explosive devices (which cause more flash than damage) are used in a variety of "Mechasports".
