    History:
      Dale grew up on Schall. His family was part of a joint fishing venture between the Schalli and humans. Dale grew up with a deep respect and admiration for the Schalli. He joined the Schalli Navy and was trained in special operations, with a speciality of long range scoutiing. After Schall joined the Dawn League in 1198, he was part of a exchange of personal between the Aubani Navy and the Schalli Navy.

      He served there for two years, when the Dawn League was reformed into the Reformation Coalition, he applied for and recieved a billet in the newly formed RCES. He gathered useful intelligence on several worlds in the Wilds, including Nova Rhyll and Xexor.

      When Assemblyman MacBaen was creating his private recovery team, he thought it would be useful to have a connection with the RCES, nothing official, but a backdoors mean of communication. With this in mind he hired Dale Green to be the navigator of the Argo.

    Personality:
      Dale defines the word enthusiasm. He is almost always upbeat and gung-ho. He loves what he is doing, exploring and trying to help people out. Dale always tries to look on the brighter side of things.
