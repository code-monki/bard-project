    History:
      Erik Earhart always dreamed of space, he grew up hearing the stories of the grand battle fleets of the Third Imperium, of people travelling among the stars as if they were just a jaunt down the street instead of lightyears apart. But as he dreamed he also learned the harsh reality, that the Imperials had thrown it all away. For this he hated them. When Senator MacBaen gave his famous "Going back" speech, Erik felt that MacBaen was talking directly to him, he immedietly went to the nearest recruiters office and signed up for the fledgling Space Force. The officers in charage quickly saw his worth as a technichan and commisioned him and gave him a job in the design team.

      He was instrumental in building and testing the first nuclear rockets that Aurora launched into space. Every few years he was given another project to work on and another promotion. But for all the project he was given, they were always based on Aurora, designing the tools and ships that others would fly. Earhart wasn't bitter about this, he knew that without him, the Navy would never work. But he always had the dream to fly on a starship as crew, to fly among the stars. In 1201, he heard that Assemblyman MacBaen, his hero of yesteryear, was equiping a ship to explore, the wilds, Earhart decided to follow his dream, and retired from the Navy and joined up with MacBaen, first overseeing the overhaul of the Argo, a recovered ship, then as its chief engineer. Maybe after a few years he would come back home, and design ships for the next generation of heroes, but for now he was going to walk among the stars.

      Earhart was never a good people person, prefering the pefection of mathematics. He is very uncomfortable around people, often stammering and loosing his train of thought. During his time in the navy he was given the call sign Earwig, and he absolutly hates it. If not at work maintaining the Argo, he will be tinkering in the workshop he has set up in his stateroom..

      Earhart has slightly balding grey hair, and is fairly stocky. He doesn't take great pains with his appearence, but is always presentable.
