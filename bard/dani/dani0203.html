Katrinka "Trinka" Dobson
Age 32 in 1201

by Lewis Roberts

    Occupation: Corporal, Oriflamme Marines
    Attributes: 57187A-8 (Adjusted for Cyberwear 76187A-8)
    Background:
      Corporal Dobson was raised in a fairly average family, in a fairly average small town. She and a group of friends went to the big city, one weekend when she was seventeen. She doesn't remember much of that weekend, but at the end of it she and three of her friends woke up at an Oriflamme Marine bootcamp. Her friends fought the enlistment without success, but Dobson found that she actually liked the marines. It finally gave her life some direction. She served with distinction, and was given a chance to join the THIRD, she leapt at the chance. A RAM grenade cost her her arm on Spencer. She simply had it replaced.
