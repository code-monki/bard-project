Charolette Stanley
Age 27 in 1200

by Alvin Plummer

    Occupation: Wealthy Traveller
    Attributes: 746777-9
    Background:
      A rather bland, stout woman, Mrs. Stanely is infamous subsector-wide as the sole survivor of the doomed shuttle Wayrith.

      The Wayrith was en-route to an insystem mining colony when an engine malfunction occurred. Because of the loss of fuel, the best the captain could do was put the shuttle in an elliptical orbit around the colony, and await rescue.

      Rescue was long in coming. Meanwhile, food ran out, and eventually lots were drawn to determine who get's eaten. Mrs. Stanley - the sole woman on-board - managed to excempt herself on the basis of her pregnacy and persuading the men not to kill her. "Imagine what the press would say! You wouldn't last a day in court!"

      When the rescuers finally arrived, only Mrs. Stanely and her newborn infant lived. When they spotted her - in a haze of bones, split for their marrow - her first words were "Look, darling! Food! Food!"

      She arrived home in a wave of sensationalism and notoriety. Aquitted of all charges, she became very wealthy and a major figure of controversy. Most of her money she blew in buying a large home in a prestigous ungerground location, but she still has enough to tour the subsector with her 6-year old son.
