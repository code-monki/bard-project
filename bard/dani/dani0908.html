    History:
      Longstreet is most interested in promoting the Federalist cause, as this is what will ensure the Freedom of the Spiri. Like most Spiri, and especially members of the Great Houses, he is not especially interested in personal gain or creature comforts, but will invest most of his gains in medical training of Spiri in Outworld Universities and Federalist political parties in other worlds.

      His immediate goals are to accumulate wealth and to learn more about Federalist parties in outworlds (i.e., which are the worthy causes to which he can donate). He will quickly contact Spiri who have assimilated into outworld society (possibly even the Spiri representatives in the Reformation Coalition), who would, because of his social status, assist him in choosing the best places to invest his money. To reciprocate he would be their spirit guide and would assist them in developing higher meditative consciousness. He spends most of his spare time either in meditation or in research of contemporary politics or into various sources related to the Lost Continent.

      Longstreet, like many members of the Great Houses, is a vegetarian. So far he has not revealed his abilities to the rest of the crew of the Argo. He understands that he might someday have to let them know about his psionic talents, but he wants to get to know them better before he does. See has decided to invest his money in stocks, 25% in Standard Astronics and 75% in Dugan & Delgado stocks. He feels that both of these companies share his politics, and will make money.

    Contacts:
      Rutherford B. See is a first cousin from your mother's brother. He is a few years older than you and as children he often lead you and your other friends on wild adventures. On the few times you got caught, he took the blame, saving you from serious trouble. He is a very happy person, with a great sense of humour. He is a powerful telepath, a few years ago he decided that he could put his gifts to better use in the Wilds, so he joined the RCES. So far they have used him as an undercover operative, where his telepathy can be used to ferret out secrets that ordinary agents couldn't get to. He has come home once since joining up. He told you of his many adventures, but you think he might have made up one or two of the stories, especially the one about the four sisters on Yontz. You don't know where he is serving right now, but you are sure he is helping make the future a better place.

      Telea Drachinloch was a fellow student at the Academy. You met in an advanced philosphy class. After graduating, she decided to stay on to work on an advanced degree in history. She later became a faculty member at the University of Gwynmark, which is probably the second best school on the planet. (Behind the Academy of course) When Spires was contacted by the RC, she got offworld, in a program to exchange faculty members with off world universities. Every six months the faculty members rotate to a new school. You think she is at the University of Dunkirk on Nike Nimbus, but you aren't sure.

    Information:
      When you were competing in the Contest, you heard several of the other contestants talking about a rumour they had heard. They were convinced that Aubaine had created a great spy network throughout the RC. The spies are on every planet, and are charged with making sure that everyone is dedicated to the RC. They also steal information that allows Aubaine to maintain its dominance in the Coalition, both technological and political. A few days later, the woman who started the conversation died in a freak accident in the Contest. Deaths in the Contest aren't unknown, but they are rare. You don't know if it was all a strange coincidence, or something else.

      You have recently heard of a new group named the Templars. They are a fighting order dedicated to killing Vampire ships and Virus infested computers. They will accept anyone who is willing to live by their rules. They do not care about past crimes or mistakes. They live under strict discipline and constantly practice weapon skills. A pair of recruiters came to Spires a few months ago, but they only managed to recruit a few people. The pair said that they have a secret base somewhere in nearby space, where they train for battle. They will not release the location of the base, in case there are agents of the Vampires in the area. The pair also spoke of a great fleet of Vampires that is gathering, and only by relentless vigilence will human space be preserved. They seemed very sincere, and very dedicated.
