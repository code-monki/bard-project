Gregor "Reaver" Kircher
Age 32 in 1201

by Lewis Roberts

    Occupation: Lieutenant, Oriflammen Marines
    Attributes: 598761-9 (Adjusted for Cyberwear 587761-9)
    Background:
      Kircher's parents were both killed in food riots in the early 70's. He was assigned to state run orphanage. Here he learned that he had to be strong to survive, any sign of weakness was seized by the other orphans. At eighteen he was turned loose, after a few months of wandering, he joined the Marines. Here he found an outlet for his violent tendencies. In fact the Marines encouraged them. He was commissioned and after distinguishing himself on an assault on a Raider's base deep in the Oort Cloud, he was offered a position in the THIRD. He jumped at the chance. He served with distinction, gathering a Purple Heart in the assault on Spencer.
