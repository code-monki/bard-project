Vangirs
Age 40 in 1201

by Alvin Plummer

    Occupation: Guide
    Attributes: 449588
    Background:
      Born into the very small Vargr community on Enos/Sword Worlds/Spinward Marches (less than 1000 menbers), he worked as a railroader for a decade before being able to buy a low passage ticket to Anduril/Sword Worlds/Spinward Marches .

      On Anduril, he worked in a sweatshop for four long years (his hands still hurt from that time) until he found his true calling as a guide to the Urgnemoff Desert. Based in the small town of Hunafloi, his archaic TL 4 tours are bringing in a constant income, allowing him to treat his friends and even import - at an outragious cost - a TL 4 balloon for his business.

      Has many connections to the local poor No blood family on Anduril: sends money back to the folks on Enos.
