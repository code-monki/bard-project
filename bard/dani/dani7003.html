Society for True Humanity

design by John H Bogan Jr

Elvis Larson, founder of the Society for True Humanity, a spiritual/self-improvement movement popular during the period of the Nth Interstellar Wars. Depending on the local activists, the movement was part religion, part philosophy, and part scam. The scams died out, the philosophy was absorbed into later movements, and the religious aspects fragmented and formed the basis for numerous local religions throughout the Solomani Sphere and regions of Solomani expansion (including the Imperium). Many of the religious sects canonized Larson as "Saint Elvis". Due to the fragmented nature of the religion, local practices vary in the extreme, often quite contradictory to practices on other worlds.
