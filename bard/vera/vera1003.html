TL-5 "Ferret" Scout Car

design by Idiot/Savant

TL-5 "Ferret" Scout Car A lightly armed and armoured vehicle typicly used for reconnaissance and scouting duties. The cargo space is typicly used to carry supplies and extra fuel for long patrols.
