GPSOR Mk. III

design by Derek Stanley and Michael Vallencourt

Nebula Entry

Incredibly common during in the Scout sevice of the 3rd Imperium the GPSOR (General Purpose Scout Operations Robot) could be found just about anywhere. GPSOR's were generally deployed as observation drones to monitor and record the lifestyles of the multitude of primitive cultures scattered throughout the Imperium. The GPSOR became particularly popular with Imperial Navel Intelligence who ulitized the drone to monitor suspected subversive organizations.

With it's small chassis (0.1 cubic meters) and exceptionally quite ducted fan propulsion system the GPSOR was considered the ideal surveilance drone.

Though most of these small unarmed robots were destroyed during the collapse the large numbers produced insured their survival into the New Era. With literally millions laying demolished around the ruins of the Imperium replacement parts are incredibly easy to come across.
