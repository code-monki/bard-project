Gladiator-Class Biplane

design by Idiot/Savant

The final development of the TL-4 biplane fighter - future aircraft taking advantage of improved internal combustion engines to propel them even faster. Due to it's high speed and low endurance, the Gladiator is best utilised as an interceptor, though it does carry a bomb rack to provide ground-attack capability.

The plane was designed with Fire Fusion & Steel.
