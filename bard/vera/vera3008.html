Arrowhead Endo-Exo-Atmospheric Interceptor

design by Nick Bradbeer

The Arrowheads form the last line of defence for Cyrinia against invasion from space. The twenty-four highly agile fighters of Guard Wing are orbit-capable and would in the last-ditch be deployed in the upper atmosphere armed with airburst nuclear rockets to destroy dropships or drop capsules. In time of peace they provide a rapid-reaction defensive force. (For more information on Cyrinia, see the Korinall Cluster (PERI0013) on the PERI page.)
