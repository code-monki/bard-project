GR-7 Gauss ACR

by Scott Quigg

The GR-7 represents a significant improvement over the existing Imperial designs. The addition of the TL-10 electronic sights greatly increases the effectiveness of the individual soldier making them an all-environment trooper without the need for WSV suite. Manufactured at TL-12 tolerances, the GR-7 is incomperable!

"Kyris has a real winner here! An assault rifle that weighs in at 4.69kg carrying 50 rounds of ammunition! Such a portable weapon with such extreme firepower is exactly what the Covenant of Suffren has been looking for!!"
