TL-5 3T Carrier-Based Fighter

design by Idiot/Savant

An early carrier-based fighter, with folding wings and a corrosion-resistant airframe and power-plant. Based on the WW II Grumman Wildcat and Hellcat.

The plane was designed with Fire Fusion & Steel.
