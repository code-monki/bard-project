Darood Lite Auto Pistol

by Jon Goff

Commonly referred to as the Darood Lite, this is a light semiautomatic pistol designed and manufactured by Darood Industries on Telnof. It fires 5.52x30mm straight rounds, they are not caseless, so they can fire rounds manufactured on lower tech worlds. The weapon is fairly inexpensive, and the ammuntion is also very common and cheap.

The Darood Industries produces a modification kit for this gun that can convert it into a Darood Dark Machinepistol. The modification switches out the barrel for a longer one, attaches a folding stock, and modifies the reciever to fire 3-round bursts. These modification kits weigh 0.864kg, cost Cr176.4, and are very easy to get ahold of.
