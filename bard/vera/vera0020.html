TL-5 8T Fighter-Bomber

design by Idiot/Savant

A twin-engine multi-role aircraft, usually used either as a light-bomber or night-fighter. As a fighter-bomber, it is equipped with an internal bomb-bay capable of holding up to 500 kg of ordenance, as well as a pair of plumbed wing hardpoints. The night-fighter variant fills the bomb-bay and some fuel space with a short-range radar gunsight. Both variants retain the heavy armament of four nose-mounted 15mm cannon and four wing-mounted 7mm machine-guns.

The plane was designed with Fire Fusion & Steel.
