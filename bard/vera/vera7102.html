Notes: A HEAP flechette round contains 8 finned darts each of which has a HEAP warhead. A discarding sabot version of this round would theoretically be possible, but even I have a limit on overkill. This design was originally conceived of as low cost weapon for stopping armored troops, but after a slight rules clarification (well change), it was no longer as effective. Still, against targets in anything but battledress, it is generally "one shot, one kill".
