Reaper Submachinegun

by Jon Goff

The Reaper is a heavy SMG that is simply a lower cost version of the Grim Reaper. Both are made by Etruscus Interstellar, a wholy-owned subsidiary of the Ides of March. The Reaper drops the silencer, the laser sight, and the telescopic sight. It is sold on the open market. Compared to its cousin, the Reaper weighs about 20% less, and costs about 50% as much. Only the ball, HE, HEAP, and flechette ammunition is commonly used.
