Regency Small Arm Rounds

design by Peter Gray

Following the Collapse, the Regency of Deneb continued its maintainment of Imperial Design Packages. This was done mostly because of the huge stockpiles of material still left over from the Imperial period. No where was this more true than with standard small arms and ammunition. To this day (090-1202) the Regency and its smaller member worlds continue to retain a number of standard Imperial weapon calibers for their military and paramilitary forces. Nowhere is this more evident than the continued use of 4mm Gauss and 7mm and 9mm ACR rounds by the lesser militaries of the Regency. Recently Regency Planetary forces have begun to move away from these standards in an effort to better the performance of their forces on the battlefields, but the sheer numbers of older weapons and ammunition found in the Wilds means that many colonists and filibusters will likely retain their old standbys for logistical reasons. Intelligence indicates that both Imperial Reserve and Vargr forces stockpile large heaps of ammo during the Vargr invasion, and many of these are still intact, making it cheaper for retention of older systems because of the easier resupply these caches will provide.
