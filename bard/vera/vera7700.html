Laser Barbettes

design by Pete Grey

Unmanned Turrets are the way to go on a smaller ship. Just one or two MFD gunners, plus the maximum possible firepower possible for the TL, can make even a scout a respectable opponent. Of course, if fire control is knocked out.....

Many of the Barbettes listed were not maxed out during the design sequence. All lasers assume a normal ROF of 40 in space combat. BPR is beam pointer and laser short range. HPG is the HPG volume.
