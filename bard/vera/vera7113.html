Koshi Submachinegun

by Jon Goff

The Koshi SMG is a military-use weapon built as part of a joint venture between McKael Metalworks and Darood Industries. It uses the same 5.52x30mm ammunition as the Darood Dark, with the addition of a TL-9 flechette round. This weapons is fairly popular due to its low recoil, decent range, and very high ammunition capacity. It is a very popular weapon among guards, members of the Fyrd scouts, and others who need decent stopping power at a reasonable price.
