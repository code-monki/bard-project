TL-5 4T Fighter

design by Idiot/Savant

The ultimate development of the single-engined fighter-aircraft at this tech level. Armed with either four heavy 15mm cannon or twelve 7mm machine-guns, as well as bombs or unguided rockets, this class of aircraft is usually used for interception or ground-attack.

The plane was designed with Fire Fusion & Steel.
