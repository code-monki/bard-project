9x20mm SMG-10

by Rupert Boleyn

An SMG made to fire the standard 9x20mm pistol round, as opposed to the 9x24mm SMG round. The idea behind this is to ease logistics by having a common round between both pistols and SMGs. It is built at TL10 as a cheap weapon to provide low-tech allies who can manufacture their own ammo for it, but still have a light handy weapon.
