P-3 Gauss Pistol

by Scott Quigg

The P-3 is the Covenant of Suffren's latest gauss pistol design. After decades of experience showed that the previous TL-10 P-1 was too heavy and cumbersome for effective combat, Kyris Industries designed a new pistol massing only 1.45kg while carrying 25 rounds.

"The P-3 is a meatshredder!! At 30 meters I can pump all 25 rounds into a small target in 5 seconds!! You can't beat that with a stick!!!" Gunnery Sergeant Kevin "Mauler" Miller, SEH.
