Biogel

design by Robert W. Ray

Biogel

TL: 10
Price: 1000Cr per L
Power Required for Nutrient Distribution: 0.0000257MW
Useful Life 24 hrs

Bio gel, also called bio-thermal gel is a nutrient-rich, arreated, organic substance which evenly distributes the heat produced by objects which it surrounds. It has half the density of water and can also be used as a nutrient pathway for living tissue.

Bio gel was originally created as an aid in tissue research. It is most commonly found in large healing vats, where it helps speed the healing process (in game terms, healing is three times faster for as long as the wounds are completely submersed for at least 24 hours). It is also used to maintain natural, organic skin which covers cybernetic limbs. In these functions, a small electrical AC charge is constantly applied

Bio gel has a usable life of 24 hours per litre, after which the gel begins to dry into small darkened lumpy masses which inhibit the conduction of the electricity needed to distribute nutrients unless removed.

Bio gel can be manufactured using the proper raw materials (found in certain combinations of food products) and a standard TL 10 fuel processing plant. The by-products of this are a green, pasty sludge and 50ml of water per 3kg of materials.
