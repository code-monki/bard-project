MGGR-13 Machinegun

by Rupert Boleyn

I decided to try and keep the weight down while getting a SR of 300m when mounted on a Tripod and using HEAP, with mixed success.I'm not sure whether it was worth the bother. At least the ammo cassettes weigh less than 60kg, so it's not impossible for a man to hump one to the gun, but clearly the MGGR-13 like other VRF guns is not really a man portable weapon system.
