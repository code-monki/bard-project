Solomani Standard Missiles

design by Rupert Boyeln

Called 'Standard' by the Confederation's Bureaucracy, they are anything but. The only standard thing about them is that they will all fit into a 10.5m3 missile tube.

Designed to fit two to a miliary 3DT turret, these were made in a bewildering number of different types - Controlled, SIM, FIM, S/FIM, short duration, long duration and everything in between.

Here are a few of the more common types:
