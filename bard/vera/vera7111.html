Grim Reaper Submachinegun

by Jon Goff

The Grim Reaper is a heavy SMG used by the Ides of March for their Talon Battledress involved in special forces operations. It was designed by the Armory and Munitions division of Etruscus Interstellar, located on Nova Terre. The Grim Reaper is quite heavy, but packs a gruesome amount of damage when using explosive rounds. This weapon is vicious enough that even armored troops will think twice before attacking someone who is armed with it. The ammunition is subsonic, allowing a sound suppressor to make the weapon silenced.
