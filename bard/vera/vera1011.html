Idoru Industries Light Excavator-SC

design by Derek Stanley

The Idoru Industries Light Excavator, affectionately known as the mini-mite, is one of the most popular single person excavators in production. With four wheel all wheel drive, and a relatively solid lifting capacity the mini-mite can be purchased with a varity of add ons allowing for the rapid transformation of the vehicle increasing it's utility from a simple excavator to a clean up device and even snow remover. This particular model, the SC, is fully sealed with complete life support allowing it's pilot to operate it an a total vacuum with out being forced to wear a p-suit, although no one ever does.
