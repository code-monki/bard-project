Darood Dark Machinepistol

by Jon Goff

The Darood Dark is a modification of the popular Darood Lite autopistol made by Darood Industries. The official modification kit adds a longer barrel, and a folding stock. It also modifies the reciever to increase the ROF to allow for 3-round bursts. The Darood Dark is a favorite among free-traders in the subsector due to the plentiful supply of ammunition, the low cost, and the decent performance. As of 1199-057 McKael Metalworks has been supplying TL-11 HEAP rounds for use with the machine pistol, as well as their Koshi Submachine Gun.
