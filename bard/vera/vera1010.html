SRC-2110 Winnie

design by Derek Stanley

This massive all-terrain vehicle is designed as a mobile base for extended ground operations. Fast and remarkable agile for a vehicle of its size, the Stonehaven Resource Collective - 2110 has been affectionately designated "The Winnie" by the troops who use her.
