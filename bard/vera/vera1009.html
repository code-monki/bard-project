Lizzie

design by Lewis Roberts

Lizzie is a emerald green armored sports car. The car is quite low to the ground and has gull wing doors. She started off life as a standard X-140 sports car, but the first owner opted for several optional features. These include twin 50 caliber machine guns (Tl-6 13mm HMG from TNE rulebook) and armor plating. The original owner shot in a card game, and the car was auctioned off to pay his debts. Matti O'Conner purchased the car and christened it Lizzie Matti was fairly happy the car, but felt it needed a heavy weapon and a rear facing weapon, so she installed a semiautomatic RAM grenade launcher in the trunk. Matti figured that if something was immune to the HMGs, then she might as well run away from it. The grenade launcher also gives her an indirect fire weapon, which she can use to soften up a target. The grenade launcher is configured to select from any one of five magazines. Matti normally equips the HMGs with discarding sabot ammunition, though if she can't get a hold of that she will use HEAP.
