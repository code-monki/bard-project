TL-7 Military Helicopter

design by Idiot/Savant

A basic light military transport helicopter, capable of carrying twelve fully equipped troops into combat, supporting them with it's machine-guns, and medevacing the casualties afterwards. Some variants add stub-wings and hardpoints to carry drop-tanks or rocket-pods.

The helicopter was designed with Fire Fusion & Steel with Antti Lahtinen's suggested adjustment of doubling rotor lift.
