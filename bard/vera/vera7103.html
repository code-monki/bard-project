Pancor Jackhammer shotgun

by Antti Lahtinen

TL-10 replica of Pancor Jackhammer shotgun build as 20 mm high-velocity grenade launcher, and equipped with gyroscopic compensator.

A hit from shotshell is resolved either as four 10-dice bursts and one three-dice burst (52 = 4x12+1x4) with DV:1 Pen:Nil, or one three-dice burst with DV:2 Pen:1-Nil per hit.
