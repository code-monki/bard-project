Hand Cannon

by Rupert Boleyn

They are based on the FrintArms HandCannon from Iain Banks' Against a Dark Background, and thus have a Flechette round. I had to cheat to get a good range for it though (I ignored the fact that they should use smoothbore barrel length requirements). Obviously in a canon game the Flechette round wouldn't exist.

Hand Cannon (lightweight)

Weapon Details
Tech Level: 12
Ammo : 10x14.8mm TL-12 straight ETC
Muzzle Energy: 1572 J
Weapon Length: 29.9 cm
Weapon Weight: 1.4393 kg loaded. 1.132 kg empty with no magazine.
Weapon Price: 360 Cr
Magazine Weight: 0.3093 kg loaded, 0.205 empty.
Magazine Price: 56 Cr (11-round grip magazine)
Ammo Price: Ball 0.37 Cr, DS HE Tranq 0.74 Cr, HEAP 1.11Cr, Flechette 1.85Cr
Ammo Weight: 9.3 g
Features: Hollow pistol grip, optic sight, double-ended magazines (for twice the price a magazine can be double-ended, so different rounds can be placed at each end, and the firer can choose which to use first). An optional Laser Sight is availible, adding 0.5 kg mass, Cr 300 to the price and reducing all recoil to 2.

Hand Cannon (heavyweight)

Weapon Details
Tech Level: 12
Ammo : 10x24.6mm TL-12 straight ETC
Muzzle Energy: 3316 J
Weapon Length: 44.9 cm
Weapon Weight: 2.3085 kg loaded, 1.877 kg empty, with no magazine
Weapon Price: 519 Cr
Magazine Weight: 0.4315 kg loaded, 0.261 kg empty
Magazine Price: Cr57 (11-round grip magazine)
Ammo Price: Ball 0.62Cr, DS,HE,Tranq 1.24CR, HEAP 1.86Cr, Flechette 3.1Cr
Ammo Weight: 15.5 g
Features: Hollow pistol grip, optic sight, muzzle brake, double-ended magazines (for twice the price a magazine can be double-ended, so different rounds can be placed at each end, and the firer can choose which to use first). A Laser Sight can be added, costing Cr300 and adding 0.5 kg to the mass (this has no effect on recoil).
