Ranger Light Battledress

design by Scott Quigg

Hyperion Heavy Industries is the manufacturer for the Covenant of Sufren's top of the line battlefield survival equipment. The Ranger Battledress is the standard light battledress for Covenant of Sufren troops.
Ranger Heavy Battledress

General Data

Tech Level: 12
Armor Value: 10
Initiative Penalty: Initive-2, Agility-1
Armor Mass: 213.5 kg
Endurance: 40hours, excepting 1hr. battery for 3km laser designator
Armor Price: 162,000 Cr
Power: Fuel Cell(.00567 Mw)
Fuel: 5.3 kg LHyd
Standard Equipment:
    WSV goggles(400m)
    30km Radio
    TL-10 Inertial Guidance System
    3km Laser Designator
    Chameleon IR Suppression
    3 km Area Jammer
    Psionic Shielded Helmet

Notes:

16.5 kg cargo; typically carries a 5.5x27.5mm/32.5 Gauss SAW w/200rnd magazine.
