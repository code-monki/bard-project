Kviir Grav Sledge

design by Antti Lahiten

Nebula Entry

The Kviir (old name for "golden eagle" of Terra) is one of the first practical grav vehicles. Kviir combines some of the features of Harrier, Nighthawk and Osprey aircraft with contra-grav technology

The Kviiris a multi-role grav sledge which uses embedded turbofan thruster and CG lifters. Since wings are not needed for lift, the Kviir uses winglets only for high-speed manoeuver. Low-speed manoeuver and hover is done with vectored thrust.

There are five versions of the basic vehicle

K1 is unarmored civilian version used for transporting cargo and passengers.

K2 is hunter/scout version with shortened hull.

K3 is armored personnel carrier, capable of carrying an 8-man squad and giving them anti-tank/anti personnel support. When seats are removed, then K-3 can carry 12 m3 of cargo, 6 stretcher patients, or 5 stretcher patients and two medics

K4 is fire-support vehicle.

K5 is heavy gunship version.

All Kviirs except the cargo version have the same basic weapon load, consisting of a 20 mm 6-barrel autocannon, two 7.62x51 mm ETC 6-barrel gatling machine guns and four 4-tube package launchers for 120 mm laser-guided anti-tank missiles,

All weapons are mounted on hull spontoons with limiter traverse, and can turn to fire up to 30 degrees off the center line.
