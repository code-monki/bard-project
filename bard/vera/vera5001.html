Virus CyberSoldiers

design by Phil O'Neill

I imagine cybernetic soldier/slaves are a big part of what's happening in Core, and I intend to use them for my campaign. Such cyber-enemies could be a great foil for player characters who insist on swapping out their own body-parts for that extra edge. Most humans in the Wilds (a little less so in the RC, and less-so to a greater extent in the still-hi-tech Regency) probably have an aversion to cybernetic enhancements, since it reminds them of the horrors visited upon civilization by Virus. Imagine how people, who are already uncomfortable with cybernetics, would react to cybered characters once Virus-cyber-soldiers are encountered.

Some suggested cybernetic implants for the typical Breed.

    TL-12
    Eye/Ear combo suite:
    WSV, Comm Receiver, Amplified sound, hi & lo frequency enhancement, plus targetting link.
    Neural jack
    Chronometer
    Inertial nav system
    Hypercharger
    Filter lungs
    Radio communicator
    Subdermal armour all round
    Utility arm
    Dynalegs
    Implant computer which uses radio link to stay in constant contact with other breed.
