Compact SMG

by Rupert Boleyn

Based on the 'Lightweight' round (10x14.8mmETC-12) from my pair of Hand Cannons , this short SMG is intended for Spec Ops and the like. Thus it is fairly expansive, has features not usually found on an SMG and is designed for maximum concealability (though it is not made out of low-profile materials like a body pistol).
