Pocket Empire Reference Information

| Home | Traveller | BARD |

BARD-PERI is the reference page for information on any pocket empires in the space surrounding the Reformation Coalition. Most of this information is not known, and only rumors filter through the Free Trader Network. Citizens who have information about known or suspected pocket empires are asked to relay this information to the nearest BARD official.
