Regency Quarantine Service Ship Classes

by Peter Gray

The Regency Quarantine Service fields a number of different ships for patrol, surveillance, inspection and logistical roles. These include hoary old designs like the Gazelle and Fiery Escorts, and their updated Praetorian and Luminous versions, the Lurushugar Killalum Patrol Cruiser, the Suleiman Scout and the old Type P Corsair. Several other ships were custom built for the Service since the Collapse, among them the Faith class Cutter, the Inspection Launch and the Trekhyair Escort. Eight other custom designs are also used:

  • Fidelity Runabout
  • Odyssey Scout
  • Morpheus Utility Ship
  • Fenris Patrol Ship
  • Khagiishan Corvette
  • Rendek Escort
  • Moneta Surveilance Ship
  • Sydkai Frigate
