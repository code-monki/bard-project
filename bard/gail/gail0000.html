Fruit of the Reformation Coalition

by Brian Mason


    Spirian Citrons
    This is a Spiri Citrus Fruit and is like a deep red navel lemon.
    Beefsteak Tomatoes
    Another Spiri fruit. Superficially it looks like a tomato, however, it actually tastes like a fine cut of meat, hence, the name. These are often baked with garlic, onion, and parmesan cheese for a traditional and filling meal.
    Seedless Cranapples
    Yet another Spiri fruit. These are a common snack and appear like hard grapes. Varieties taste more or less like the Ocean Spray drink. Varieties have different predominant flavors (apple vs cranberry) depending on soil acid content.
    Ruby Red Grapefruit
    Probably the produce Spiri take most pride in, this is the only strain of produce brought from Earth which would thrive on Spires.
    Vezian Chiromoya
    Very similar to the fruit of Chile, however, this one is sweeter and seedless.
    Galosian Plantains
    Actually closer in taste to bananas, however, they are quite large, often 1.5 meters in length.
    Sunrise Melon
    "As sweet as the melons of Zloff" is a phrase still in the vernacular, and with good cause. This most popular of Eosian fruit has a light blue husk, going through pink, red, and orange to the core of yellow. The seeds are also quite nice, and taste like sunflower seeds, but have been genetically engineered to be sterile to prevent this from growing offworld.
    Blood Orange
    From Nike Nimbus, this deep red Orange is similar in taste to the more typical variety of orange. It is a natural aphrodisiac when cooked for about 4-5 hours at 400K.
    Luhtalan Grapes
    While primarily renowned for their wine, this older variety of grapes not used for wine is quite popular. The taste is more similar to muscadines.
    Dewmellon
    From Ra, specifically, the community of Sendegon. The Dewmellon is small (8 cm diameter), but quite tasty.
    Melban Peaches
    Melban peaches come from the Isle of Melba on Aurora. These sweet tangy fruits are a favortie Aurora.
